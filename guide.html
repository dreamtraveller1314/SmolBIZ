SmolBiz — How to Use This Website

SmolBiz is a small business management web app. It's built for two kinds of accounts: Admin (the business owner/manager) and Worker (staff). What you can see and do depends on which role you signed up or were invited as.

1. Logging In / Signing Up

  • Admins sign up directly and create their business profile.
  • Workers join through register with the email their admin invites them with — they don't self-register from scratch.

2. The Sidebar Menu

Once logged in, you'll see a sidebar. What's on it depends on your role:

Admin sidebar Admin

  • 🏠 Home — dashboard overview of the business (health score, sales snapshot)
  • 🧾 Sales & Products — record sales, manage product list
  • 💸 Expenses — log and track business expenses
  • 📊 Analytics — charts/insights on how the business is doing
  • 🤝 Collab & Trend — see announcements/collab opportunities from other businesses
  • 💬 Chat & Calendar — team chat plus an auto-generated meeting calendar
  • 👥 Worker Management — invite, view, manage your workers
  • ⚙️ Settings — business profile settings

Worker sidebar Worker

  • 🏠 Home — worker's own dashboard (e.g. attendance, tasks)
  • 💬 Chat & Calendar — same team chat + calendar as admin
  • ⚙️ Settings — personal account settings

3. Chat & Calendar

This is a shared feature for both admins and workers.

  • Channels work like group chats — admins can create new channels (e.g. "Kitchen team").
  • Messages update live between everyone in the channel — no need to refresh.
  • An unread dot appears next to a channel (and in the sidebar) if there's a message you haven't seen yet.
  • Smart scheduling: if you type something like "Meeting next Monday at 4pm" in chat, SmolBIZ automatically detects it and adds it to the shared team calendar — no manual entry needed.
  • Switch to the 🗓️ Calendar tab (within the same page) to see upcoming and past events. Admins can edit/delete events; workers can only view.

4. Sales & Products Admin

  • Add products with price, stock, and a low-stock threshold so you get warned before running out.
  • Record each sale against a product — SmolBiz tracks quantity and payment method.

5. Expenses Admin

Log business costs separately from sales, so your profit calculations stay accurate.

6. Analytics Admin

A visual breakdown of sales, expenses, and overall business health over time.

7. Collab & Trend Admin

A space where admins from different businesses can post announcements or look for collaboration opportunities.

8. Worker Management Admin

  • Invite new workers by email.
  • Set permissions per worker (e.g. access to sales, products).
  • Remove workers if needed.

9. Settings

Admins manage business-wide settings (business name, logo, etc.); workers manage their own personal account info and avatar.

10. Notifications

SmolBiz plays a sound and shows an unread dot when a new chat message comes in on a channel you're not currently viewing, so you don't miss anything while working on another page.
